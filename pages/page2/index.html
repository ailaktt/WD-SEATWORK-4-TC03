KEN

Played by Ryan Gosling

Ken doesn't have the same amazing attributes as Barbie, and he doesn't
even have a job, his job is just "beach." His romantic advances are
constantly rejected by Barbie, but that doesn't stop him from continuing
to try to pursue her. He ends up joining Barbie on her journey to the real
world where he learns about the patriarchy and that the real world treats
him far better than Barbie Land does.
